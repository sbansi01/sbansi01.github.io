About Us:

In Hindi, SAPNA means "dream." It was our family's dream come true to bring SAPNA to our adopted community of Patterson Park. We are proud to share hearty and authentic Indian dishes straight from our family's recipe book. We strive to be a place where people feel welcome and come together in their enjoyment of Indian cuisine.
